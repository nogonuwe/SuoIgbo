bu - to be

A bu m Anyi bu
Ị bu Unu bu
Ọ bu Ha bu

funanya - to like

A fulu m oloma anya (I like oranges) Anyi fulu oloma anya (We like oranges)
Ị fulu oloma anya (You like oranges) Unu fulu oloma anya (You all like oranges)
Ọ fulu oloma anya (He/She/It likes oranges) Ha fulu oloma anya (They like oranges)

choo - to want

A cholu m (I want) Anyi cholu (We want)
Ị cholu (You want) Unu cholu (You all want)
Ọ cholu (He/She/It wants) Ha cholu (They want)

nwee - to have

E nwelu m (I have) Anyi nwelu (We have)
Ị nwelu (You have) Unu nwelu (You all have)
Ọ nwelu (He/She/It has) Ha nwelu (They have)
